Simple Ways To Improve Your Mental Health.

by eric share

The most prudent way of tackling any problem is to first define the problem.

According to WHO, mental health is a state of well-being in which the individual realizes his or her own abilities, can cope with the normal stress of life and can work productively and fruitfully, and is able to make a contribution to his or her community. It is the self-actualization of one’s intellectual and emotional potential.

Mental health is a vital part of life as it plays an important role in our overall wellbeing.

How is it so? Someone may ask. Well, the relationship between the mind and the body is so intimate, the health of one has a great impact upon the other. Your thoughts, emotions, attitudes, behavior etc, has a great impact upon your overall health, and mental health means having a sound mind with sound thoughts, emotions and so on, which intern becomes beneficial in promoting your overall health.

Now, having defined what mental health is and knowing what role it plays in our lives, it’s time now we learn how to attain to it and to grab all its benefits upon our lives.

Laugh Often

While being physically healthy can improve one’s mental health, having ailments make it nearly impossible for one to attain or even maintain mental health. Laughing has a tremendous benefit upon our health. It raises the levels of B lymphocytes and T cells, which are the active agents in the body’s immune system thus helping the body to fight infections. Laughing also releases a special hormone (endorphins), which act as the body’s natural pain killer.

Looking at the future with hope

Anticipation of the future optimistically reinforces self-confidence promoting one’s ability of performance. Our thoughts have great impact upon our reality. When one continually cherish a pessimistic mindset, his or her life can be miserable. The body always tend to respond to thoughts and dwelling upon positive thoughts can work on your side. Placebo effect.

Good Memories

Retrieving experiences, anecdotes and events from the past that bring about relaxation helps reinforcing hope in the future and also promotes optimistic view of our surrounding.

Avoid Negative Emotions

Hatred, envy, rage, frustration, pessimism, sadness, impatience, despair, distrust, discontent, anxiety, all work to break the life forces and invite decay and death. Therefore you don’t want to cherish such thoughts if you want to attain to mental health.

Trusting in GOD

Having hope and trust in GOD increases confidence while facing any circumstances in life. God calls us to rejoice evermore and to give thanks in everything. Also we are to be anxious for nothing but in everything by prayer and supplication we are to make our requests known to Him whose eyes are upon the sparrows.

Gems Of Wisdom

Thou wilt keep him in perfect peace, whose mind is stayed on thee: because he trusteth in thee. Trust ye in the LORD for ever: for in the LORD JEHOVAH is everlasting strength:
